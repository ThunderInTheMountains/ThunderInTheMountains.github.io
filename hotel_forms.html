--- layout: info title: Hotel Vendor Forms ---

This page is for Vendors requiring Hotel services for electrical, phone, or other miscellaneous services.

Credit Card Authorization Form - This is a required form by the hotel to confirm any additional services (electrical/phone, etc.)

Electrical Form - This is for any electrical outlets you may need from the hotel for your vendor booth

Phone/Internet - This is for hard line phone hookups, or any internet services you would require for your vendor booth.

Miscellaneous - This is for other services and or special permits for vehicle parking (RV, Semis, etc.)
